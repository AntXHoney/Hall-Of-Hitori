Bocchi The Rock!

An anime television series adaptation produced by CloverWorks aired from October to December 2022. A second season has been announced. Both the manga and the anime received widespread critical acclaim for the animation, writing, voice acting, comedy, characters, music, and exploration of social anxiety.

Hitori Gotoh

This is Hitori Gotoh, a student with social anxiety but wants to have lots of friends. She might not look like it, but she plays the guitar exceptionally well! Known as "Guitar Hero" in her youtube. Due to her anxiety she cannot talk to another person(with the exception of her family) in the eye. Until one fateful day, she met a drummer from another school Nijiki Ichiji. Nijika was looking for another guitarist as the previous one ran away due to "skill issue" hence she has joined Kessoku Band!

Nijika Ichiji

An energetic and cheerful second-year high school student. She is the drummer of Kessoku Band. The organizer of her band who takes care of Hitori was already joined Kessoku Band. Nijika was a fan of "guitarhero", and eventually realizes that she was actually Hitori, but this makes her become more confident of achieving her dream. Until it was revealed to everyone by Poison♡Yami, she kept it a secret from other members. Although she has her limits with Bocchi as of late due to the latter's self esteem where she felt that Bocchi needs to grow a spine.

Kita Ikuyo

A bright, popular, and "socially powered" first-year high school student. She is the guitarist and lead vocalist of Kessoku Band. Originally, she joined the band and then ran away because she lied about knowing how to play the guitar just to be near Ryo, whom she admires deeply. After Hitori helped her overcome her guilt and taught her the basics, she rejoined with a new determination. Her "Kita-aura" is so bright it often overwhelms the introverted Hitori, yet she is incredibly kind and acts as the band's social bridge. She is the most active member on social media and constantly looks for ways to make the band more popular, all while diligently practicing her guitar to keep up with Hitori's talent.

Ryo Yamada

A cool, eccentric, and fiercely independent second-year high school student. She is the bassist of Kessoku Band and the one who composes the music. Ryo is known for her calm demeanor and her tendency to spend all her money on vintage bass guitars, often leaving her "broke" and eating weeds to survive. She was the first person to truly appreciate Hitori’s "Guitar Hero" skills from a technical standpoint, even though she often teases her. Ryo previously belonged to another band but left because she didn't want to compromise her artistic integrity, eventually forming Kessoku Band with her best friend, Nijika. She enjoys her "loner" status and often encourages Hitori to embrace her own weirdness rather than trying to fit in.
